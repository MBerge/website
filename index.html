About Me

Reykjavík, IS

I design with
intent.

Thirty three years ago I started my career as a freelance web developer and editor for a literary magazine. I’ve since worked across automotive, real estate, travel, airline, universities and creative industries — always anchored by the same question: does this help a real person do a real thing?

Iceland’s landscape taught me more about UX than any framework. Honesty of materials. Deliberate emptiness. Contrast that earns its place. I bring that sensibility to everything I touch.

Currently freelance. Previously at Marel Garðabær in the software division leading the UX team.
